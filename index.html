Majaheem

They are called Najd camels, and they are characterized by their dark color, large size and abundant milk production. They are not used for riding or carrying except in necessity.

Maghatir

Maghatir or Al-Wadh, which is medium in size, and its average production of milk, and its white color distinguishes it with an aesthetic appearance

Al-Shaqhaa

Al-Shaqhaa less white than clear, and it is one of the Maghateer types. Its milk production is average

Al Shaalaa

It has overlapping colors between red and blond. Its milk production is average.

Al-Safar

It is distinguished by its abundance and fluff, and its color is a mixture between white and red.

The Red Camel

The red camel is distinguished by its medium size and its low milk production.

Iwark

It is also called a camel camel, its size is medium to small, its milk production is medium with light hair, and it was named by this name because of its abundance in the areas where Iwark trees grow.





Canned camels

They are purchased for grazing, fattening, milk production, and transportation in the desert. Their price ranges between 15,000 and 25,000 Saudi riyals. The price is determined according to the age of the camel, its health, physical strength, size, and weight. The price also varies from female to male.

Al-Mazayen camels

their price is many times higher than the prices of ordinary camels, as their prices range between one and two million Saudi riyals, and may reach more than that, and the word “Mazayen” means camel beauty competitions. It is spread in the Arab Gulf countries such as Saudi Arabia, Qatar and the Emirates, and the competition is for the title of the most beautiful camel, and the value of prizes in some competitions may reach 10 million riyals.
